About the Owner

About the Owner

My name is Angelica Del Rosario, and I am currently 51 years old. I was born in Pampanga and am currently living in Makati. I have a stable job of being an accounting manager and had this business as my hobby and a source of some income.

My passion started and was discovered during my teenage. I started doing haircuts and makeup, where I would experiment with how to do my own makeup and would cut my own hair in front of a mirror. The passion didn’t die out but was forgotten when I entered high school. I studied for a college degree then I realized that I had a passion for being an artist – A makeup artist. It extended by studying in a school of arts for hair and makeup. After that, I decided to open a business. A men’s and women’s salon.
